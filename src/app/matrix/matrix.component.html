↓ Instructions ↓

  • 1. You can draw any pattern using your mouse.
  • 2. You can also choose patterns from the options on the right side of the screen, which will be displayed on the grid.
  • 3. To clear the grid use the "Reset" button.
  • 4. Press "Play" to start reproducing the game indefinitely.
  • 5. Press "Pause" to stop the reproduction.
  • 6. Use the "Next Step" button to watch the reproduction step by step.
